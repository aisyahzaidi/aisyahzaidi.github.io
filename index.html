Being a good Muslim

Through the course of my study, I was able to understand the significance of increasing knowledge in becoming a pious Muslim. This Usrah has defined Islam, Iman, and Ihsan in the life of a Muslim.

The important of adab

I also learned the value of etiquette (adab). In usrah, I had learned about the virtues of etiquette, and how to use them in my daily life. I discovered that constant self-development based on the knowledge imparted improves manners, piety, and faith.

Knowledge, skills and understanding

As a student, I also gained in engaging in communicating, giving opinions, meeting new friends, working together to complete group tasks, debating religious affairs and current concerns, being responsible, reminding myself to increase good deeds and remember the day of judgement.
  • In the usrah, I have also studied reflections of manners in students' personalities which talk about the manners that need to be instilled in a student in socializing with friends, lecturers and other people. What is important is that students need to constantly improve their self-discipline and manners in communicating to become excellent students in this world and the hereafter
  • I understand the importance to study religious knowledge more deeply through this usrah. For me, a student must take the initiative to gain a deeper understanding of Islam by learning new information, such as by watching a short lecture about Islam.
  • Many lessons can be taken from the stories of the previous rasul and prophets to teach Muslims today. Therefore, I realized the importance of reading and taking advice from the stories of previous prophets to become a Muslim who obeys the commands of Allah SWT.

As a first-year student, I thoroughly enjoyed my first Usrah! These are some activities
and tasks I completed throughout the Usrah this semester. I'll discuss many types of activities briefly. 
Apr 3, 2023
Online (usrah)

One of the activities was made online through Google Meet by our naqibah. I do enjoy how Sister Adlene gives lectures and bits of advice in her Usrah. The topic chosen is 'Adab in Learning Knowledge'. She tells us how are the correct way to interact with the lecturers in their classes and other manners which opened my mind to become a student who takes care of manners in terms of speaking, behaviours and so on. She also advises correcting the intention because Allah SWT in studies.

May 19, 2023
Class Face To Face

This picture is taken in our first meeting of Usrah class held at Mahallah Hafsa Surau. Face-to-face learning is more fun because you can interact with new friends and meet Naqibah (Sister Adlene). "Ihsan and Eschatology" was the topic of discussion that evening. Our naqibah addressed the meaning of Ihsan, Ihsan in worship, the meaning of eschatology, Islamic eschatology, and the ten signs of the end of the world in lectures. She also interpreted several Quranic verses. That night, I learned numerous things, such as Ihsan in worship, because Allah likes people who do good.

May 22, 2023
Last activity

Our last usrah. This is our final lesson and activity for the semester. It feels both sad and delighted to see them for the last time. I hope to see them again, especially sister Adlene, in the coming semester. Our naqibah instructed us to separate into 5 groups and assign various topics to each group. 'Manners with Neighbours' was the title given to my group. The interesting thing is that my group enjoys doing this exercise while getting to know one another. We collaborate to complete assignments and brainstorm ideas. That night, I learned several things, such as how to interact with my parents and neighbours.

May 22, 2023
The end of usrah

This is our last usrah meeting this semester. Our naqibah has talked about a topic which is 'Sustainability in Islam'. There are several interesting points in this last topic which is 'Sustainability in Islam' emphasizes responsible resource use, environmental care, and future generations' well-being through ethical use and environmental care. To create the identity of a true Muslim, every student, in my opinion, needs to be made aware of this topic.

ROLEPLAY


My group and I accomplished an assignment involving roleplaying. This project is about how we share knowledge and understanding of the selected topic. My group chose the Qur'an's Two Stories, particularly the stories of Qarun and the Seven Sleepers. My group consists of four members: myself (Farah Nur Aisyah), Imtiaz Nabahah, Nur Alya Dania, and Nurkauthar. Nurkauthar and I told the narrative of Qarun. My task is to convey the beginning of Qarun's story as related by Allah SWT in the Quran. Next, Imtiaz Nabahah and Nur Alya Dania delivered the story of The Seven Sleepers. The roleplay must be presented in usrah class with the guidance of our naqibah, Sister Adlene. Our usrah class was held via Google Meet while my group was doing a presentation. As a first-year student at IIUM, this is my first usrah. The assignments not only provided me with knowledge and ideas, but they also assisted me in meeting new people. I manage to get communication skills, and leadership abilities, generating ideas with teammates, being responsible, and working together to complete the tasks assigned to us.

TASK 1

Next, my naqibah, sister Adlene, prepares several tasks every week, such as watching short Islamic movies. This task is one of the weekly actions in usrah, but it contains different tasks. Our naqibah assigned us the first task of watching the movie and writing down what we learned or what struck us the most. This video tells the story of a Bani Israel woman who works as a prostitute. Indeed, this story has changed my mindset on not taking lightly the smallest good deed done because it could be the cause of our entering Jannah. I believe that Allah SWT will reward even the smallest act of compassion performed with sincere intentions. Allah SWT grants mercy to his servant for him to continue doing good. We must help any human or animal in need. What touched my heart was Allah's mercy for His servant, regardless of the wrongdoing that a servant has committed. I realized that a sincere person's goodwill can lead someone to goodness.

TASK 2

This activity takes place throughout the month of Ramadan. Sister Adlene, my naqibah, has given a Islamic's video and comments on how we individually feel about this month, as the activities have already been completed. The month of Ramadan is a glorious month and a happy month for Muslims all over the world. This month is also quite challenging for me as a first-year student at IIUM. Every day is very tiring because of the tight class schedule, but I enjoy it and it feels good to be able to fast and enjoy the fasting month here. Based on the video provided, I feel this month is the most special, and I believe every Muslim feels the same. After watching the short Islamic video given, I realized a few things, like how effective my actions are during the month of Ramadan and what changes benefit me. As a student, I realized that it is important to reflect on oneself to become a Muslim who believes and is pious. For me, the month of Ramadan is a good month to increase rewards, improve oneself and become a more obedient servant to Allah SWT.
